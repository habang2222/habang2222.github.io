What this category is for

This page is the home for bug bounty work: methodology notes, vulnerability classes, reporting examples, and lessons from recon or triage. It gives the category its own route so later posts have a proper landing page.

It can also hold checklists for finding issues, notes on target scope, and summaries of how I improved my testing process over time.
